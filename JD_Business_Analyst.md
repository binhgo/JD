# Business Analyst (BA)

## About the Company

We are a growing pharmacy marketplace company revolutionizing the way pharmaceutical products are distributed and accessed. Currently operating a successful B2B platform, we are expanding into the B2C space this year to directly serve end consumers. As we embark on rebuilding our technology infrastructure from the ground up, we are looking for talented individuals who thrive in dynamic, fast-paced environments and want to make a meaningful impact in the healthcare industry.

---

## Job Summary

We are seeking an experienced and self-driven **Business Analyst** to join our team during a critical phase of our digital transformation. In this role, you will be instrumental in gathering business requirements from stakeholders across the organization and translating them into comprehensive documentation that enables our development team to build scalable, user-centric solutions. This is a unique opportunity to shape processes from scratch and directly influence the architecture of our next-generation marketplace platform.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- **Requirements Gathering:** Conduct stakeholder interviews, workshops, and discovery sessions across departments (operations, logistics, sales, customer service) to collect, analyze, and consolidate business requirements.

- **Documentation:** Translate business needs into clear, actionable technical specifications including Business Requirements Documents (BRDs), Functional Requirements Documents (FRDs), user stories, and acceptance criteria.

- **Process Design:** Analyze existing workflows related to warehouse operations, logistics, and marketplace transactions; propose optimized, scalable solutions that support both B2B and B2C business models.

- **Stakeholder Communication:** Serve as the bridge between business stakeholders and the development team, ensuring requirements are clearly understood and properly implemented.

- **UI/UX Collaboration:** Create wireframes, mockups, and visual representations of requirements to facilitate discussions and align stakeholders on product vision.

- **Process Establishment:** Define and implement BA best practices, templates, and processes as we build our documentation framework from the ground up.

- **Continuous Improvement:** Identify opportunities for process optimization and recommend solutions that enhance operational efficiency and support long-term business growth.

---

## Required Qualifications

- **Experience:** 5+ years of experience as a Business Analyst, preferably in e-commerce, marketplace, logistics, healthcare/pharmaceutical industries.

- **Domain Knowledge:** Strong understanding of warehouse operations, logistics workflows, and marketplace business models. Experience in pharmaceutical or healthcare industry is highly desirable.

- **Self-Starter Mentality:** Proven ability to work independently, take initiative, and establish processes with minimal guidance in ambiguous environments.

- **Requirements Expertise:** Demonstrated experience conducting stakeholder interviews, facilitating workshops, and consolidating requirements from multiple sources.

- **Documentation Skills:** Excellent ability to create clear, comprehensive technical documentation (BRDs, FRDs, user stories, process flows) that development teams can readily implement.

- **Analytical Thinking:** Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to analyze complex business processes and propose scalable solutions.

- **Communication Skills:** Exceptional verbal and written communication skills with the ability to explain complex requirements to both technical and non-technical audiences.

- **UI/UX Aptitude:** Ability to create wireframes or UI mockups using tools such as Figma, Sketch, Balsamiq, or similar to visualize requirements.

- **Collaboration:** Team player with excellent interpersonal and presentation skills; comfortable working with cross-functional teams.

---

## Preferred Qualifications

- **Technical Background:** Prior experience in software development, coding, or engineering roles is a plus.

- **Database Knowledge:** Familiarity with databases, SQL, and data structures to better understand technical constraints and possibilities.

- **Agile Experience:** Experience working in Agile/Scrum environments and using tools like Jira, Confluence, or similar.

- **Healthcare/Pharma Experience:** Direct experience in pharmaceutical distribution, pharmacy operations, or healthcare technology.

- **B2B & B2C Experience:** Previous experience with both B2B and B2C business models and their unique requirements.
